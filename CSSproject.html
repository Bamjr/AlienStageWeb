ALIEN STAGE

What is Alien Stage?

Alien Stage is an ongoing series of animations on YouTube led by VIVINOS in collaboration with QMENG and produced by STUDIO LICO.

Plot

Alien Stage, a reality audition program, has taken the alien world by storm, and its upcoming season is planned to be just as exciting. Contestants must sing for their survival, as the loser faces on-the-spot termination. As the competition heats up, a mysterious girl emerges, whose involvement may hold the key to humanity's fate...

Songs

Blink gone -"Blink Gone" is an original song featured in Alien Stage's 7th/Final Round and performed by AKUGETSU and BL8M - the voices of Till and Luka, respectively. The song and episode were released simultaneously on October 25th, 2024.

Cure - "Cure" is an original song featured in Alien Stage performed by AKUGETSU and BYEONGHOON - the voices of Till and Ivan respectively. The song was first introduced alongside the release of ROUND 6. It later received a remix cover on September 30th, 2024, in celebration of ALIEN STAGE's 2nd Anniversary. It was performed by C!naH and Rubyeye - the voices of Sua and Mizi, respectively.

Ruler of my heart - "Ruler Of My Heart" is an original song featured in Alien Stage sung by BL8M and Rubyeye - the voices of Luka and Mizi respectively. The song was first introduced on April 28th, 2023 with the release of ROUND 5. It later received a remix cover on December 31st, 2023, sung by C!naH, the voice of Sua. On October 6th, 2024, it received another remix cover for Alien Stage's second anniversary - this time sung by 6FU;, the voice of Hyuna.

Black sorrow - "Black Sorrow" is an original song featured in 'Alien Stage OST Part.4' sung by Park Byeong Hoon, the voice of Ivan. The song was first released and introduced on January 5th 2023 with the release of ROUND 3. It later received a remix cover on September 7th 2023, sung by Rubyeye - the voice of Mizi.

MY CLEMATIS- "MY CLEMATIS" (나의 클레마티스, Naui Keullematiseu) is an original song featured in 'Alien Stage OST Part.1' composed by Team JL and performed by Rubyeye and C!naH - the voices of Mizi and Sua respectively. The song was first introduced in ROUND 1. It later received a remix cover on September 7th 2023, in celebration of ALIEN STAGE's 1st Anniversary, performed by AKUGETSU and BYEONGHOON - the voices of Till and Ivan respectively.

All-in - "All-in" is an original song featured in the Alien Stage OST produced by STUDIO LICO and sung by 6FU;. It later received a remix cover on September 21st, 2024, in celebration of ALIEN STAGE's 2nd Anniversary, performed by AKUGETSU, the voice of Till.

Unknown (Till The End...) - "Unknown (Till The End...)" is an original song featured in 'Alien Stage OST Part.3' composed by XENO VIBE & BL8M and sung by AKUGETSU - the voice of Till. The song was first introduced on October 10th, 2022 with the release of Round 2. Later on, it received a remix cover on December 31st 2023, sung by AKUGETSU and Rubyeye, the voice of Mizi.

Sweet Dream - "Sweet Dream" is an original song featured in 'Alien Stage OST Part.2' composed by XENO VIBE & BL8M and sung by C!naH - the voice of Sua. The song was first introduced on September 20th 2022 along with the release of the Prologue. Later on, it received a remix cover on September 7th 2023, sung by BL8M - the voice of Luka.
